Colors

By Default when a link isnt clicked, aka unvisited, it is blue and underlined.
When it is clicked, aka visited, it is purple and underlined.
And when its currently being clicked, aka active, it is red and underlined.
However, these can be changed, and you can make spectacular things with it such as buttons.
