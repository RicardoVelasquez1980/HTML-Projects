Healthy vs Unhealthy

Foods typically rich in nutrition and have helpful benefits to them are considered healthy foods. These can range from organic food to GMO food.
Foods with low nutrition and high sugar, fat, and calories are considered unhealthy foods. These can range from fast-food to snacks.

Pros & Cons

Healthy

Consumption of healthy foods gives you plenty of energy so you can do all the tasks for the day. However, plenty of energy isn't the only great thing about healthy food.
It also leads to a improved mental health, gut health, and heart health. We also can't forget that healthy foods make you less prone to cardiovascular diseases.
So if healthy foods have such amazing benefits why doesn't anyone just eat healthy? Healthy food is more expensive than unhealthy food, so a majority of the lower class tend to eat unhealthy.
In addition to the higher cost of healthy foods, most people don't find it tasteful enough.

Unhealthy

Consumption of unhealthy foods releases endorphins so consumers temporarily recieve feelings of euphoria. Unhealthy food is also everywhere and affordable for anyone, this makes it the go to when hungry.
However, just because the food is tasty and makes you feel good, it doesn't mean it is good for you. Unhealthy food gives you very little energy so you may end up not doing all which you plan on doing for the day.
Low energy for the day isnt the only thing that comes from eating unhealthy. Obesity, heart and cardiovasular diseases, lack of focus are all negative outcomes of unhealthy foods.
